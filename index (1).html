Save Our Planet

Vehicles are America's biggest air quality compromisers, producing about one-third of all U.S. air pollution and are the biggest contributor to global warming. The smog, carbon monoxide, and other toxins emitted by vehicles are especially troubling because they leave tailpipes at street level, where humans breathe the polluted air directly into their lungs.
